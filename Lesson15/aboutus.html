Who Am I?

Hello! My name is Mariana Rodriguez. I am 22 years old and I am Mexican. I am currently a student with a strong interest in technology, engineering, web design, and electronics.

I first became interested in programming during high school, where I studied programming systems and learned basic concepts. That experience motivated me to continue developing my skills, which led me to study Computer Systems Engineering at UAT in Mexico.

During my studies, I explored different areas of engineering, including electronics, electricity, robotics, and circuits. I also gained experience working with databases using SQL and applied my knowledge to real projects, such as designing a timed irrigation system.

My interest in engineering comes from my desire to contribute to meaningful change and help make everyday life easier through technology. I enjoy solving problems and creating practical solutions that can improve people’s lives.

I also enjoy web development because it combines logic and creativity. I like designing user-friendly interfaces, adding interactive features, and creating visually appealing experiences for users.

This website represents the skills and projects I have developed throughout this course.

My Learning

During this course, I learned how to build websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and jQuery. I also learned how to structure web pages, create navigation menus, and improve user interaction through animations and effects.

One of the most valuable parts of this class was understanding how design and programming work together to create functional and professional websites. I learned the importance of organization, usability, and visual design in web development.

Professional Goals

My goal is to continue developing my technical and creative skills so I can work in technology or engineering fields in the future. I am especially interested in roles that combine design, systems, and problem-solving.

I believe technology has the power to improve the world, and I hope to be part of that change by contributing to meaningful and innovative projects.
